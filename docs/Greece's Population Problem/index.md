# The problem with Greece's population
***

[<img src="../assets/articles/greece/greece-aging-population.jpg" >](https://www.ekathimerini.com/economy/1266242/greece-bracing-for-demographic-shift-as-aging-accelerates/)
<br>
Greece famously has problems with its economy. If it devoted the entirety of the money made in the country in the span of a year to the cause of paying off its debt, it would still take all of it and more. A slightly lesser known problem Greece has is with its aging population; it is the third oldest in the EU after Italy and Germany. Greece has an elderly population that makes up 19.4% of its total.
